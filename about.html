Who we are

Aelix Studios is a student-led design studio focused on building mobile applications that help learners feel more confident, creative, and empowered.

Our mission

Supporting student success

We create tools that reflect the way students think, study, and connect with their communities.

Our story

Two students building a premium, friendly brand experience.

We started Aelix Studios to bring student needs into every step of the design and product process. Our focus is simple: build useful mobile apps with polished details and thoughtful interactions.

What we care about

Clarity, empathy, and design that feels intuitive. We want every screen to feel calm, capable, and easy to use.

What we build

Mobile experiences for students, with features that support planning, creativity, and everyday learning.

What’s next

More student-focused projects, a richer blog for community updates, and future tools that help learners thrive.

Team

Two builders, one shared vision.

We're keeping things lean and focused. With a small team, we move quickly, learn from every release, and make every detail count.
