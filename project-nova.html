Experience Design — 04

Project
Nova

An immersive brand and platform launch for a creative services partner — rich visuals balanced with usability-driven structure.

Type Experience Design
Scope Brand Identity & Web
Deliverable Creative Platform
Year 2026
Research

Customer-first positioning.

Before touching a single pixel, we spent time understanding who the brand was actually speaking to — and whether the current identity was reaching them. Through visual direction exercises and messaging audits, we aligned the product with the right audience and the right context, giving every design decision a clear reason to exist.

Visual Design

Distinctive digital identity.

A bold visual palette and elegant typography help Nova stand out while remaining calm and approachable. We built contrast into every level of the hierarchy — between type and space, between motion and stillness, between what the brand says and how it says it — so the experience rewards attention without demanding it.

Results

A platform built for growth.

The final delivery included flexible page templates, a motion-ready layout system, and a content framework designed for future releases. The brand now has a digital home that reflects its ambition — and the tools to keep evolving it without starting over.
