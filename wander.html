Loops always return to your exact start; with an end point set, arrival there is guaranteed — the scenic detour is sized to your duration. Google Maps opens the route with waypoints; Apple Maps links support a single destination, so that link targets your end point (or the loop's midpoint). GPX carries the exact route for any navigation app. On Apple Watch, share the GPX to Komoot or WorkOutDoors for wrist turn-by-turn — Apple's own Workout app can't follow routes, so the Apple Maps button is the Apple-native fallback (its directions mirror to the watch).
