Overview

One hub for everything we make.

As the studio's catalogue grows, keeping every app installed, updated and easy to reach shouldn't be the user's job. The Aelix Launcher is our answer: a single desktop application that discovers, installs and updates everything we ship. Open one window, and all of our work is a click away.

Trust

Verified, straight from the source.

Every download the Launcher performs comes directly from our public GitHub releases — no third-party mirrors, no bundled extras. What you install is exactly what we published, and the Launcher itself is open source, so anyone can read precisely what it does.

Details

Small touches, considered.

Updates happen quietly in the background. Installs are one click. And because downloads occasionally take a moment, there is a tiny arcade tucked inside for while you wait — we believe even the utility software should have a little soul.

Status

Available now, evolving constantly.

The Launcher is live and free for Windows 10 and 11. It ships alongside every new Aelix Studios release, and its roadmap grows with the studio — web apps like Wander are reachable from it today, with deeper integration on the way.
