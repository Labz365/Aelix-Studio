Overview

Designed to feel premium, friendly, and intuitive.

Aelix Study is our first mobile application — built from the ground up with students in mind. Every screen, every interaction, every piece of copy was written by people who understand what it means to be overwhelmed by deadlines, distracted by noise, and in need of a tool that simply works. The result is an app that feels calm, confident, and completely at home in a student's daily life.

Navigation

Student-first flows.

Simple, consistent layouts let learners move quickly through tasks without friction. We stripped out everything that wasn't serving a purpose — no decorative chrome, no buried settings, no feature bloat. What remains is exactly what a student needs, exactly where they expect it.

Visual Design

Calm, refined, approachable.

Soft tones, generous spacing, and neutral accents keep the experience from feeling clinical or cold. We drew on editorial design principles — the kind of care you find in a well-set book — to make sure that even a task list feels considered. The app doesn't shout. It invites.

Next steps

Built on real feedback, built to grow.

We are using early student feedback to refine the experience and shape our next set of features. This is only the beginning — the first product from a studio that plans to keep making tools that make campus life better, one thoughtful release at a time.
