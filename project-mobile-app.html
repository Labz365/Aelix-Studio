SF
Me

A student budgeting app built to make financial clarity effortless — because money stress shouldn't get in the way of learning.

Type Mobile Application
Platform iOS & Android
Focus Student Budgeting
Year 2026
Overview

Budgeting, designed for the way students actually live.

SFMe is our first mobile application — a student budgeting tool built from the ground up with real financial pressures in mind. Tracking spending, splitting costs, understanding where money goes: things students need to do but rarely have a good tool for. SFMe makes all of it feel effortless, without sacrificing the quality of the experience.

Design

Calm, clear, and built for daily use.

Financial apps tend to feel either intimidating or childishly simple. SFMe sits in neither camp. Clean layouts, considered typography, and a restrained colour palette give the app a premium feel while making every number easy to read. It is designed to open quickly, communicate clearly, and get out of the way.

Technology

Cross-platform, built in Flutter.

SFMe is built with Flutter and Dart — a single codebase that runs natively on both iOS and Android. This means students on any device get the same polished, performant experience. Flutter's expressive UI toolkit lets us maintain tight control over every interaction, animation, and layout decision.

Status

In active development.

SFMe is currently in development. We are building in the open, iterating on real student feedback, and shipping incrementally. This is the first product from Aelix Studios — and the foundation for everything we plan to build next.
